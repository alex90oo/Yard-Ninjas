About Yard Ninjas

Yard Ninjas is run by three local teens looking to make a difference in our community! We started this business to help support our parents and pay for school while providing top-notch outdoor and car detailing services to our neighbors.

We believe in hard work, attention to detail, and turning every yard and vehicle we touch into something you can be proud of. Thank you for supporting a small, local team!
